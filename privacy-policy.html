Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

NAEVO Studio ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect when you visit naevostudio.com or purchase from us, how we use it, and your rights regarding it.

We collect information you provide directly to us, including:

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We only share information with:

We retain contact and order information only as long as necessary to fulfill orders, respond to inquiries, and meet basic accounting/legal obligations. You may request deletion of your data at any time (see Section 6).

Our website does not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies at this time. If this changes, this policy will be updated accordingly.

6. Your Rights
